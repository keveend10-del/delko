## The Problem in One Paragraph

Bernadette opened in November 2024 with one of the strongest chef origin stories on the North Shore — Raymond Blanc, Bar Boulud, 2-star New York Times. The product is real. The room is beautiful. The press showed up (Boston Globe, Boston Magazine, Northshore Magazine, all within six months). And yet: 33 Yelp reviews, 7 TripAdvisor reviews, 4,755 Instagram followers, and a slow summer in a city that receives 2.2 million visitors a year. The problem isn't the restaurant. The problem is the marketing.
