# Sales Scripts

## Cold Outreach — First Touch

**DM / Text / Email:**

Subject (email): Quick question about [Business Name]

---

Hey [Name] — I run a local marketing agency called Delko focused entirely on [North Shore / Berkshire County] businesses.

I looked up [Business Name] and noticed a few things that might be costing you calls. Happy to put together a quick, free breakdown — no charge, no pitch.

Worth a look?

— Jack

---

## Follow-Up (Day 3 if no reply)

Hey [Name], just circling back. I put together a quick note on what I found for [Business Name] — took about 10 minutes and it's pretty straightforward. Want me to send it over?

---

[Name], here's what I found for [Business Name]:

**What's working:**
[1–2 things]

**Biggest gap:**
[Main issue in one sentence]

**Quick wins I'd fix first:**
1. [Action]

## Proposal Close

"Based on what you told me, here's what I'd recommend:

[Package name] — [price/mo]

That covers [3 main deliverables]. Given your average job is [X], if we get you just [1–2] more jobs per month, the service pays for itself.

Month-to-month. No contract. If it's not working in 60 days, you can leave.

Want to move forward?"

---

## 30-Second Core Pitch (memorize this)

"Delko is a local marketing agency for [North Shore / Berkshire County] businesses. We help contractors and service businesses get more calls from Google, get more reviews, and follow up with leads automatically — so they're not losing jobs to competitors who just show up better online. Most of our clients see movement in the first 30–60 days."
