# Delko 2026 Business Playbook

## Who We Are

Two-person local marketing agency serving North Shore MA and Berkshire County businesses.
Founders: Keveen Delgado and Jack Koutrobis.

One-line pitch: We help local businesses get found online, look more trustworthy, and follow up with leads faster so they turn more searches into real customers.

---

## Core Positioning

We help local businesses get more calls, quote requests, bookings, reviews, and repeat customers by fixing the marketing foundation: Google Business Profile, website, reviews, local visibility, lead capture, and follow-up.

**AI/GEO angle (practical, not overhyped):**
People are asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini who to hire. If your business information, reviews, website, and local authority are weak, you may not show up or may be represented poorly.

---

## 90-Day Goal

Get first paying clients and proof. Do not hide in planning.

- Days 1–7: Finalize offer, site, scripts, audit/proposal templates, CRM, target list
- Days 8–21: Outreach sprint
- Days 22–45: Close first 2–3 clients, document proof
- Days 46–75: Systemize SOPs and reporting
- Days 76–90: Decide best niche/package, push toward $5k–$10k MRR
